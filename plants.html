All About Outdoor Plants

Why Plants?

Having worked for the largest Garden Centre Chain the UK has ever known, I have made it my mission to bring the joy of outdoor plants to everyone.

I've also had the privilidge to work with Award Winning Garden Designers for show gardens - so I've learnt a thing or two about plants!

Seasonal Beauties

We hunt down the horticultural beauties of each season in order that you can look forward to a sensational selection of outdoor plants in your balcony garden!

Opitune Moments

I want you to receive your plants when the time is just perfect. What this means for you is that I plant up your insert and ensure the plants have grown to the perfect point so that you receive your garden before it looks amazing (Maximum enjoyment for you)!

The Plant Bug

No, it's not dangerous... but it's very contagious! When you embark on the plant journey, you can't help but learn from your plants! Every season is diferent, but don't worry - there will be botanical stories, facts and of course care guidance with deliveries!
