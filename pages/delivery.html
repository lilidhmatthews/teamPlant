ALL ABOUT OUTDOOR PLANT CARE

Plants Are Alive!

So many people that I've talked too are fearful of keeping their plants alive. However it really is simple - if you've been out in the wind, you're sure to have a bad hair day! Plants are the same but they are tenacious and bounce back quickly!

It's Not Cheating

What is special about the pot is that it's designed to tell you how much water your plants have! Keep an eye on the MIN - MAX level! If it's been raining then you need to check your plants aren't being overwatered. If it's really hot, you'll need to top up more often. Not scary right?

Care Guidance

You really won't be on your own! Each delivery comes with guidance on your new plants. The more you learn about your plants, then you'll be able to give them the best care!

We want to share plant knowledge! The more plants in cities the better it is for our health, city greening and nature itself! Just wait, it won't be long until the ladybugs, bees and butterflies to begin visiting!
