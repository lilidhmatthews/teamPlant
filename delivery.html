All About Outdoor Plant Delivery

There's Not Enough Space!

When I first moved to London, I tried to grow plants on my balcony. I even had a tarpaulin for when I re-potted plants in my living room. I would have to hide plants that weren't in season, bags of compost and the tools. There just wasn't enough space on my small balcony! So I thought, what if I could have a new garden every season? Not only would it always have seasonal interest, I wouldn't have the hassle or mess!

Do you deliver to me?

We currently deliver to London. However we are always looking to expand and deliver to new areas. Get in touch to see if we are coming to you soon!

When do you deliver?

Select your delivery date when you sign up. Your second delivery will be exactly 3 months from your first delivery (for the next season!). If you want to change your next delivery date then pleace get in touch! We want to make sure that you only pay for what you receive.

What if I'm not in?

You're time is important! We know that you're not always going to be at home, which is why we ask you to name a safe delivery space! Your first delivery will be waiting for you there when you get home! Then for any subsequent deliveries, simply wheel your pot back to this space and we will do a direct swap for you, leaving you with a new season's worth of plants!
